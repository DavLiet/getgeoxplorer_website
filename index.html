Introducing GeoXplorer

GeoXplorer is an Augmented Reality application that you can use to "Xplore" 3D terrain from Earth, the Moon, and Mars!

This app is part of a larger initiative in Washington University in St. Louis's Department of Planetary Science, where we are using AR to bring the outside world into the classroom. Learn about the Fossett Lab for Virtual Planetary Exploration.

Coming to Google Play soon!
